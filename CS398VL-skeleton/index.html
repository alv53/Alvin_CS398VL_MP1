About this Page

There are 2 main measurements of word importance used in the web page. The first is just pure frequency. The frequency of the word (one graph for normalized, and one without normalization) is one way of showing the significance of the word in the book. The other method implemented on this page is TF-IDF, or term frequency - inverse document frequency.
